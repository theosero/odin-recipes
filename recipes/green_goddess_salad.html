Green Goddess Salad Recipe

Green Goddess Salad

A simple romaine salad topped with classic green goddess dressing made with mayonnaise, parsley, spinach, basil, tarragon, and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 cup chopped parsley
  • Basil leaves
  • Fresh chives - chopped
  • Tarragon leaves
  • Medium shallot
  • Small garlic clove
  • Anchovy paste
  • Fresh lime
  • Medium ripe avocado

Steps

  • Chop and combine the green ingredients
  • Add anchovy paste and squeezed lime juice
  • Blend the mixture until smooth
  • Add mayonnaise and salt, blend till mixed
  • Combine cabbage, scallions, and cucumber in a large bowl
  • Add dressing and toss till even coating is achieved
  • Top the bowl with avocado and serve
